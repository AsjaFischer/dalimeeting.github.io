How to get to Sestri Levante


Shuttle Service: we are organizing a shuttle service from Genova Airport to Sestri Levante for participants arriving on March 29th and leaving on April 2nd. The shuttle schedule will be posted soon on this page.


Self Organized Travel: if your travel plans do not coincide with the shuttle schedule here are our suggestions on how to get to Sestri Levante:

  • From Genova Airport: taxi or "volabus" shuttle service to Piazza Principe train station in Genova. Then take a direct train to Sestri Levante (about 1/1:15 hour trip).
  • From Pisa Airport: shuttle service to Pisa Centrale train station. Then take a direct train to Sestri Levante (about 1:30 hour trip).

Trenitalia Website: to organize your train trip in advance we suggest you to consult the italian train service website at this link.
